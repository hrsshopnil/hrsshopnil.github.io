Account Deletion – Qbank BD

Users of the Qbank BD mobile application can request deletion of their account and associated data by following the steps below.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Qbank BD app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings
  3. Select Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion request

If you are unable to access the app, you may request account deletion by contacting us via email.

Request Deletion by Email

Send an email from your registered email address to:

qbankbdapp@gmail.com

Please include the subject line:
“Account Deletion Request – Qbank BD”

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • User account information
  • Authentication data
  • Quiz progress and activity
  • Leaderboard data

Data Retention

All personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days of the deletion request, except where retention is required by law.
