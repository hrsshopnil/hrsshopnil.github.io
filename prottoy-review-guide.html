📘 Prottoy Quiz Review Guide

Last updated: February 2026

This guide explains how to review quizzes inside the Prottoy Admin Panel and maintain question quality.

🔐 Access Admin Panel

  • Open the provided link
  • Login using your credentials

📂 Open Assigned QBank

  1. Go to the QBank tab
  2. Select your assigned QBank
  3. View the quizzes listed under it

📊 Quiz Status

  • 🟡 Yellow Clock → Needs review
  • ✅ Green Tick → Reviewed

👁️ Review Questions

  1. Click the Eye (👁️) icon
  2. Check:
    • Question text
    • Options
    • Correct answer
    • Explanation

🔀 Reorder Questions

  1. First unlock the reorder system by clicking the Reorder Unlock button.
  1. Then drag the reorder handle to rearrange 3–4 questions.

🏷 Assign Chapter

  1. Select the correct chapter
  2. Click Update

🧹 Keep Explanations Clear

  • Remove lengthy or repetitive explanations like this in the image above
  • Keep them short and directly related to the answer
  • Add explanations only when necessary
Clear explanations improve understanding. Unnecessary text reduces clarity.

✏️ Edit Incorrect Questions

  1. Click the Pen (✏️) icon
  2. Fix text, options, answers, or explanation
  3. Click Update

✅ Mark as Reviewed

  • After checking all questions, click “Mark as Fixed”

⚠️ Review Rules

  • Do not skip questions
  • Every question must have a chapter
  • Verify answers before finishing

🎯 Objective

Ensure accurate, clean, and high-quality question banks for students.
